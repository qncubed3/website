Issues connecting the CAS to your PC

1. Is your CAS charging after plugging it into your PC? If not, your cable may be faulty and you'll need to find a replacement.
2. There have been instances where a Mac does not recognise a CAS. Try a windows PC or another PC.
3. If you don't have any other options, you can ask your tutor after class for assistance.
